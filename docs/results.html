Critic Score

Strongest predictor by far. Each 1-SD increase in critic score adds ~9 points to user rating.

Sports Genre

Sports games are rated 4.7 points lower on average compared to other genres.

Platform Effects

PS2 shows positive effect (+2.7), while PC games trend lower (-4.3).

Release Year

Older games rated higher, likely due to survivorship bias.

Model Comparison

Wide & Deep MLP

✓ Strengths

  • Slightly better RMSE (10.6)
  • Flexible architecture
  • Captures non-linear interactions
  • Educational manual implementation

⚠ Limitations

  • Black box predictions
  • No uncertainty estimates
  • Harder to interpret
  • Requires more tuning

Bayesian Regression

✓ Strengths

  • Interpretable coefficients
  • Uncertainty quantification
  • Clear feature importance
  • Statistical rigor

⚠ Limitations

  • Assumes linear relationships
  • Slightly higher RMSE (10.9)
  • Slower training (MCMC)
  • Sensitive to feature scaling
